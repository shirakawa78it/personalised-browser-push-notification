Journey Builder Custom Activity

Your Journey Builder Custom Activity is now deployed!



The custom activity should be available within Journey Builder. You can follow these steps to locate it.
  1. Login to your Salesforce Marketing Cloud account
  2. Navigate to Journey Builder under the Marketing Automation pillar.
  3. Click on the 'New Interaction' button or select an existing interaction.
  4. The custom activity should be located at the bottom of the left hand panel, under the 'Custom' label.
